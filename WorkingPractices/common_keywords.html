Commonly-Used Keywords

The following table lists some common keywords you may sometimes see:

Keyword

Usage

Notes

collab:

Indicates that the ticket has been authored

by someone who is not 100% employed

by the Met Office

Use form collab:<orgname>

e.g. collab:niwa, collab:oxford

blocks:

Code change is blocking the numbered ticket

from going on to the project’s trunk

e.g. blocks:#1234

Not used by LFRic as they have a “blocked by”

item in their ticket options.

blockedby:

Code change is blocked by the numbered ticket

from going on to the project’s trunk

e.g. blockedby:#6789

Not used by LFRic as they have a “blocked by”

item in their ticket options.

kgo

Indicates the change requires new kgo

installing (change in answers);

See KGO.

Includes when a new job is added to the

project’s rose stem suite

macro

Indicates the change includes an

upgrade macro

doc

Indicates that the change includes

documentation updates

SR:<name>

Denotes person who will SciTech

review the change

Optional for reviews outside of the SSD

team; Added later in development

CR:<name>

Denotes person who will CodeSys

review the change

Added only by SSD team

um

jules

lfric

socrates

casim

ukca

Indicates that the change has a linked

ticket with the code base specified by

the keyword.
